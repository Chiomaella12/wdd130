ABOUT US-RAPID

Here at the Rapids, our white water rafting trips are designed to provide thrill-seekers with the most unforgettable experience on the water. Our expert guides ensure safety while you navigate through stunning landsacpes and challenging rapids. Oars Up!

History

Rapids started floating the river in 2000. we have been providing unforgettable experiences for over 30 years. we are a family owned and operated business that is dedicated to providing you with best experience possible. we and our team are ready to have you.

Today, rapids welcomes visitors from around the world to enjoy thrilling river adventures and breathtaking scenery. Our experienced guides, modern equipment, and dedication to environment stewardship ensure that every trip is both exciting and memorable for guest of all ages.
